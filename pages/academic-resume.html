The ALM capstone direction is tightly coupled to the SNPTX systems engineering work and is supervised by the assigned capstone advisor.

SNPTX: deterministic orchestration for multi-modal biomedical ML

Advisor
Steven Elston · CSCI E-108 instructor (fall 2025) and assigned ALM Data Science capstone advisor.
Sequence
Precapstone in Fall 2026 (proposal and methodology framing); Capstone in Spring 2027 (defense and final writeup).
Direction
Reproducibility and provenance harness for multi-modal biomedical ML workflows, with content-hashed inputs, deterministic Snakemake DAGs, MLflow-logged runs, and per-experiment lineage. Capstone deliverable centered on a single bounded scientific surface (current candidate: a reproduction-and-provenance pass for a published graph-based therapeutic-prediction model) rather than a horizontal framework demo.
Inspection surface
All artifacts and pages designed to be reviewed end-to-end without chasing follow-up materials. The reviewer-facing entry point is snptx-academic/#for-review.
