Closed-loop process

Selection, execution, classification, update

The autonomous engine operates as a closed loop within the experimentation subsystem: it selects configurations via adaptive defaults and meta-analysis, executes pipeline runs, evaluates outcomes against catalog history, classifies findings as `new_best`, `novel_config`, or `surprise`, and updates campaign-level confidence scores for subsequent rounds.
