Argument

Why infrastructure depth matters

The reproducibility crisis in biomedical ML is not caused by model quality. It is caused by uncontrolled analytical drift, implicit state dependencies, absence of structured model comparison, and missing provenance. SNPTX addresses each root cause architecturally rather than procedurally.
