Design philosophy

How the document handles strong and weak results

Where results are strong (imaging at 99.3%, drug discovery at 97.5%, single-cell at 96.9%), we report them. Where results are constrained by data (clinical text at 61.0%), we report those equally and explain the boundary. The value proposition is disciplined infrastructure: transparent experiments, auditable comparisons, and reproducibility controls within validated scope.

Honesty of scope

Why this section matters

The limitations section protects the rest of the document from overclaiming by explicitly documenting where evidence is thin, partial, or still theoretical.

Practical implication

What the reader should infer

SNPTX is strongest as a pilot-ready research framework with clear infrastructure discipline, not as a proven production or clinical deployment platform.

Continuation logic

How future work is framed

The future directions list extends directly from present gaps, which makes the roadmap look like the next technical layer of work rather than a detached wishlist.
